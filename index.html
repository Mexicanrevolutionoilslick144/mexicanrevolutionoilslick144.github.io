Download Dictator for Windows

Dictator converts your voice into text quickly. It handles your words and improves them with help from advanced language models. You keep control of your data and your workflow stays private.

💡 What this tool does

  • Speech to Text: Dictate your thoughts and see them appear on your screen.
  • Smart Cleanup: The app fixes grammar and structure in your notes.
  • Hotkeys: Use simple keyboard shortcuts to start and stop recording.
  • Privacy First: Your clipboard and data remain on your machine.
  • Direct Insertion: The text drops right into your active program window.

⚙️ System Requirements

Dictator runs on Windows 10 or Windows 11. Your computer needs a working microphone and a stable internet connection to process audio files. You will need roughly 200 MB of free storage space for the installation.

📥 How to install

  1. Visit the release page found here.
  2. Locate the latest Windows installer file ending in .exe.
  3. Click the file to download it to your computer.
  4. Open the file from your Downloads folder.
  5. Follow the screen prompts to finish the setup.

🚀 Using the App

Once you open Dictator, the app runs in your system tray. Click the icon to configure your settings.

Setup Keys: You must provide your own API key for the speech processing service. Paste your key into the settings menu to enable cloud features.

Taking Notes: Press your chosen global hotkey to begin recording. Tap it again to stop. The app converts your voice to text and places it into the program window you currently have open.

🛡️ Privacy and Safety

This software respects your data. The code processes audio through your own private API key. It does not store your voice on external servers. The clipboard history stays local to your device.
